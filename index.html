Digital Boundaries

The Practice:

  • Set specific "no phone" hours (e.g., 8-9 PM family time)
  • Turn off non-essential notifications during deep work blocks
  • Create tech-free zones (bedroom, dining table)
  • Schedule dedicated "deep work" blocks (90-120 min) without digital interruptions

The Science:

Research from UC Irvine shows that constant connectivity depletes mental energy by up to 40%. Gloria Mark's studies reveal it takes an average of 23 minutes to refocus after an interruption.

Gratitude Loop

The Practice:

  • Document one professional win each day
  • Write down what you learned from challenges
  • Acknowledge colleagues who helped you succeed
  • Review your wins weekly to track growth

The Science:

Research shows daily gratitude practice increases resilience by 23% and reduces imposter syndrome by creating a positive feedback loop.
